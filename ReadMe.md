# StudyBuddy

AI-powered learning platform for exam preparation.

## What is this?

StudyBuddy helps students study smarter by using AI to create personalized learning plans. Upload your syllabus, get tested on what you know, and receive custom study materials.

## Features

- Upload syllabus and get it organized automatically
- Take quizzes to find your weak areas
- Get AI-generated summaries and practice questions
- Personalized study schedule based on your exam date
- YouTube video recommendations for each topic
- Audio summaries for studying on the go
- Track your progress

## How to use

1. Create an account
2. Upload your syllabus
3. Take the assessment quiz
4. Follow your personalized study plan
5. Study with generated materials and resources
